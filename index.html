Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

HSAN Studios ("we", "us", "our") operates the ARC3D™ software and website at hsan-studios.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account:

  • Name — used for your account profile and correspondence
  • Email address — used for account login, confirmation, and support
  • Password — stored as a SHA-256 hash; we never store your plaintext password

When you make a purchase, we also collect:

  • Last four digits of your card number — for your reference only
  • Card expiry date and cardholder name (hashed)
  • Payment method (Card or PayPal)

We do not store full card numbers, CVV codes, or any raw payment credentials.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Data is stored in two locations:

  • Locally on your device — using browser localStorage and IndexedDB for offline access
  • Cloud server — when you are logged in, your account data and project files are synced to our secure server (MongoDB, encrypted in transit via HTTPS) so you can access them from any device

If the server is unreachable, the application works fully offline using local data. Clearing your browser data will remove local copies but cloud data remains accessible by logging in again.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and manage login sessions
  • Process and record purchases
  • Send confirmation and invoice emails (via your device's email client)
  • Provide customer support when you contact us

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

5. Cookies & Tracking

ARC3D™ does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We use browser localStorage to maintain your session and preferences. No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts are embedded in the application.

6. Third-Party Services

ARC3D™ does not use advertising networks or analytics platforms. When you are logged in, data is transmitted to our own secure server for cloud synchronisation. Payment processing is handled by PayPal under their own privacy policy.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — view all data stored about you (available in your Dashboard)
  • Rectification — update your name and email via your Dashboard
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data via the Dashboard "Danger Zone"
  • Portability — your data is stored locally and accessible to you at all times
  • Objection — contact us if you have concerns about data processing

You have full control over your data at all times. Local data is on your device; cloud data can be deleted from your Dashboard or by deleting your account.

For our full UK GDPR-compliant data protection policy, see Data Protection & GDPR Policy.

8. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (server) and SHA-256 (local)
  • Cardholder names are hashed before storage
  • Full card numbers and CVV codes are never stored
  • All server communication uses HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • API endpoints are protected by JWT authentication, rate limiting, and security headers

9. Children's Privacy

ARC3D™ is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@hsan-studios.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the website or in-app notification. Continued use of the Software after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
